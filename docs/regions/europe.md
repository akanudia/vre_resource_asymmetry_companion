# Europe

R10 macro-region covering the EU, UK, EFTA, and non-EU South-Eastern Europe.
See Extended Data Table 1 in the manuscript for the exact composition.

## Physical setting (brief)

Europe is the canonical heating-driven temperate region: winter heating
dominates demand seasonality; winter storm-track wind drives positive
wind–demand seasonal alignment; solar–demand seasonal alignment is
negative (summer-peak solar, winter-peak demand). The macro-region sits in
the upper-left quadrant of the Fig 4b alignment landscape. Within-region
wind resource quality is moderate; spatial heterogeneity is lower than
continental-scale macroregions like North America or China.
